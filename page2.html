About

  During my time at the University of Texas at Dallas, I was able to enroll in various ITS courses that provided me with essential skills from educating me about methodologies utilized in the business world to using many instances of software that helped my technical expertise. Some key courses include: ITSS 4351- Foundations of Business Intelligence, where I gained many insightful skills in data visualization as well as learn about key concepts regarding business intelligence. 
    
  Additionally, I further expanded my knowledge and expertise by taking several other courses that complemented the skills gained from Foundations of Business Intelligence. In ITSS 3390 - Web Design & Development, I acquired proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, enabling me to create interactive and user-friendly web interfaces. Moreover, ITSS 4340 - Enterprise Resource Planning introduced me to SAP, providing insights into managing complex business processes within organizations.

  Furthermore, ITSS 4381 - Object Oriented Python Programming equipped me with Python skills, empowering me to develop scalable and efficient applications. In ITSS 4300 - Database Fundamentals, I honed my skills in both SQL and NoSQL databases, allowing me to work with various data management systems effectively.

  Lastly, in ITSS 4370 - Information Technology Infrastructure, I gained expertise in working with AWS, specifically EC2 and S3, enabling me to understand and utilize cloud computing for business solutions.

  The combination of these additional courses, along with the knowledge gained from Foundations of Business Intelligence, has provided me with extensive experience and a diverse skill set in data analytics, data visualization, software development, and cloud computing. Through hands-on projects and assignments in these courses, I have honed my abilities and can confidently apply these skills to real-world scenarios, making me a valuable asset in any business setting. With these skills, I aspire to apply them to a future career in consulting, troubleshooting, or cloud solutions within the IT sector.
